Our Story

A Passion for
Authentic Flavors

Founded in 1987 by Chef Marco Bianchi, Bella Cucina has been serving authentic Italian cuisine in the heart of the city for over three decades. Every recipe is a carefully preserved secret, passed down through generations of the Bianchi family.


We source our ingredients directly from small farms and artisan producers across Italy — from San Marzano tomatoes to aged Parmigiano-Reggiano — ensuring that every plate carries the true taste of Italy.
